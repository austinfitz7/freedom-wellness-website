The Story

Built on Freedom

We're Austin & Samantha Fitzgerald — veterans and entrepreneurs based in Merrimack, NH. We served our country and came home with a mission to build something of our own. That started with Freedom Vending LLC, a growing vending machine operation across New Hampshire and Massachusetts.

But as we grew Freedom Vending, we kept seeing the same thing: people wanted better options. Not just for snacks and drinks, but for their health. Energy drinks that weren't loaded with garbage. Supplements that actually worked. And as the conversation around GLP-1, peptides, and longevity science started exploding, we knew there was an opportunity to bring something real to our community.

That's how Freedom Wellness was born. Same mission as everything else we build — give people real solutions, delivered with integrity, by people who actually care about the outcome.

We're not a faceless online store. We're your neighbors in Merrimack. When you reach out, you get us — not a bot, not a call center. Just a real conversation about what you're trying to accomplish and how we can help.
